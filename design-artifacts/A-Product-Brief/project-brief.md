## Project Scope

A web platform (mobile later) where users list used books either for exchange — for a specific book or open to any book — or list them for direct sale.

---

## Challenge / Opportunity

People accumulate books they no longer need, but there's no easy, dedicated way to trade or offload them — so the books sit unused instead of finding a new reader.

---

## Design Goals

- **Low-friction listing** — posting a book should be fast and easy, not a chore.
- **Quick matching** — users should find an interested trade/sale partner quickly, before listings go stale.
