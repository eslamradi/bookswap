## Scenarios

### [01: Tom's Five-Minute Declutter](01-toms-five-minute-declutter/01-toms-five-minute-declutter.md)
**Persona:** Tom the Declutterer — Fear: listing effort outweighs payoff, abandons (15/15, HIGH — top force overall)
**Pages:** Sign Up/Login, Batch Listing, My Listings/Dashboard
**User Value:** Lists 8 books in under 10 minutes, box empty, no more thinking required
**Business Value:** Supply engine for Goal 1.1 (2,000 listings in 6mo)

---

### [02: Priya's Race Against the Deadline](02-priyas-race-against-the-deadline/02-priyas-race-against-the-deadline.md)
**Persona:** Priya the Deadline Swapper — Want: fast yes/no on match existing (14/15, HIGH)
**Pages:** Home/Landing, Search Results, Notifications
**User Value:** Definitive answer within seconds, alerted the instant a match appears
**Business Value:** Validates Goal 1.2 (40%+ match rate); captures high-intent lead via notify-me

---

### [03: Maya's Treasure Hunt](03-mayas-treasure-hunt/03-mayas-treasure-hunt.md)
**Persona:** Maya the Bookworm — Want: discover unexpected books (12/15) / Fear: condition worse than described (13/15)
**Pages:** Browse Listings, Book Listing Detail, User Profile/Reputation, Create Listing (single)
**User Value:** Finds an unexpected gem, trusts what she'll receive, completes a swap
**Business Value:** Core retention loop — feeds Goal 2.1 (satisfaction) and 2.3 (60%+ second listing)

---

### [04: Tom's Safe Handoff](04-completing-the-trade/04-completing-the-trade.md)
**Persona:** Tom the Declutterer — Fear: sharing home address with a stranger (13/15) — also resolves Maya/Priya's ghosting fears
**Pages:** Trade/Offer Flow, Messaging, Checkout, Shipping/Handoff Coordination, Trade Confirmation & Tracking, Post-Trade Rating
**User Value:** Ships without exposing his address, closes the trade in a few taps
**Business Value:** Protects Goal 2.2 (<5% disputes) and 3.3 (<10% need support) — shared trust infrastructure across all personas

---

### [05: Tom Locks Down His Privacy](05-toms-privacy-settings/05-toms-privacy-settings.md)
**Persona:** Tom the Declutterer — Fear: sharing home address with a stranger (13/15), addressed proactively
**Pages:** Account Settings
**User Value:** Sets privacy defaults once, never has to think about it mid-trade
**Business Value:** Reduces early-funnel privacy-driven drop-off, supports Goal 3.3
