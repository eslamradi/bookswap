## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Confirm to Tom that his books are live and the task is genuinely done — this is the scenario's success point and the emotional payoff for his effort.

**User Situation:** Just bulk-submitted 8 books, expecting confirmation the box is now "handled."

**Success Criteria:** Tom sees all 8 listings live, feels the task is complete, and closes the app without needing to do anything else.

**Entry Points:**
- Batch Listing (1.2) — only entry, immediately after bulk submit

**Exit Points:**
- None required for scenario success — Tom may close the app here
- "Add more books" → back to Batch Listing (1.2), for a future session
- (Shared, out of scope for this scenario) claimed listings lead into Tom's Safe Handoff, Scenario 04

## Page Sections

### Section: Confirmation Banner
**Purpose:** Deliver the emotional payoff — clear, immediate confirmation the task succeeded.
**Content:** "✓ 8 books listed!" — count matches exactly what was submitted, no delay/ambiguity.

### Section: Listings Summary
**Purpose:** Let Tom scan the list to confirm everything looks right, without requiring him to act on anything.
**Elements per row:** Thumbnail, title, status badge ("Live"). No edit/delete affordances prominent on this first view — Tom's job here is done, not begun.

### Section: Add More (secondary)
**Purpose:** Low-emphasis path back to Batch Listing for a future decluttering session — not pushed, just available.

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |

| Listing claimed (future visit) | Another user claims a book | Status badge changes from "Live" to "Claimed — action needed" | Tap through to Trade/Offer Flow (Scenario 04) |
