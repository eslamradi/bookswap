## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Get Tom from "never used bookswap" to "ready to list" with the absolute minimum friction — this is the first test of his abandonment fear (15/15, top force in the Trigger Map).

**User Situation:** Just finished a Saturday closet clean-out, box of ~8 books in front of him, phone in hand. Low patience for "setting up one more app."

**Success Criteria:** Tom has an active account and lands on Batch Listing in under 60 seconds, with zero required fields beyond authentication.

**Entry Points:**
- Search result for "sell my old books online" (external, primary)
- Referral link from a friend (external, secondary)

**Exit Points:**
- Batch Listing (1.2) — the only forward path

## Layout Structure

Single-column, no navigation chrome — a distraction-free auth screen. Nothing on this page should tempt Tom to browse or explore; the only goal is get-in-fast.

## Page Sections

### Section: Value Reinforcement
**Purpose:** Confirm to Tom, in one line, that he's in the right place — reduces bounce before auth even starts.
**Content:** Single headline tied to his trigger, e.g. "Get your books gone in minutes." Not generic marketing copy.

### Section: Authentication
**Purpose:** Get Tom authenticated with the fewest possible taps.
**Elements:**
- Social login buttons (Google, Apple) — one tap, no typing, primary path
- Email fallback — single field + continue, no separate password-creation step (magic link or OTP, not a password to remember)
- No profile setup, no "tell us about yourself," no optional fields of any kind

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Default | Page load | Auth options visible, headline visible | Choose auth method |
| Loading | After tapping an auth method | Brief spinner on the chosen button only | None — auto-advances on success |
| Error | Auth fails (e.g. network) | Inline message below the button, method stays selected | Retry same method |

---

## Technical Notes

- No password creation flow — Tom's abandonment risk is proportional to every extra field. Magic-link/OTP email or social OAuth only.
- This page must not show any marketing/upsell content beyond the one-line headline — every additional element is a potential drop-off point for this specific persona.
