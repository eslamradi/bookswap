## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Let Tom coordinate handoff details with the other user without exchanging personal contact info or an address directly — the core privacy-preserving mechanism of the platform.

**User Situation:** Just accepted the trade, needs to sort out logistics before shipping.

**Success Criteria:** Handoff logistics are agreed without either party ever seeing the other's phone number, email, or home address in plain text.

**Entry Points:**
- Trade/Offer Flow (4.1) — after accepting

**Exit Points:**
- Checkout (4.3)

## Technical Notes

- Consider automated detection/masking of phone numbers, emails, or addresses typed into messages, as a safety net beyond just "not showing" real contact info elsewhere in the product.
