## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Settle transaction terms — payment collection for the sale path, or a simple pass-through confirmation for a pure exchange.

**User Situation:** Handoff logistics agreed in Messaging, ready to finalize.

**Success Criteria:** Payment (if applicable) is settled with no friction; pure exchanges pass through instantly with no unnecessary steps.

---

## Page Sections

### Section: Transaction Summary
**Purpose:** Show exactly what's owed (or that nothing is), no surprises.

### Section: Payment (sale path only)
**Purpose:** Standard payment collection — should not introduce new friction beyond what's needed for a simple, low-value transaction.

### Section: Pass-Through (exchange path)
**Purpose:** For pure exchanges, this should feel like a single confirming tap, not a "checkout" in the retail sense.

---

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Sale — payment due | Direct-sale trade | Amount + payment method selector | Pay |
| Exchange — no payment | Pure book-for-book trade | "No payment due" confirmation | Continue |
| Payment failed | Card declined etc. | Inline error, retry | Retry payment |

---

## Technical Notes

- Payment processor integration is a dependency for the sale path only — exchange-only launches could ship without this page needing real payment logic (pass-through confirmation only).
