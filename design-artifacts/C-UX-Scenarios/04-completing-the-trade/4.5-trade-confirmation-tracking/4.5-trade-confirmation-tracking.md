## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Let both sides track the shipment so neither is left wondering whether the other followed through — this directly addresses the cross-persona ghosting fear (Maya 11/15, Priya's "paid into a swap that never ships" 11/15).

**User Situation:** Package shipped (or waiting to be shipped), wants reassurance it's actually moving.

**Success Criteria:** Both parties see live, trustworthy tracking status without needing to ask each other "did you ship it yet?" in Messaging.

**Entry Points:**
- Shipping/Handoff Coordination (4.4) — after generating a label and shipping

**Exit Points:**
- Post-Trade Rating (4.6) — once delivery is confirmed

## Page Sections

### Section: Live Status

### Section: Key Dates
**Purpose:** Shipped date and estimated delivery, set clear expectations for both sides.

---

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Awaiting shipment | Label generated, not yet scanned by carrier | "Waiting for drop-off" status | None — passive wait |
| In transit | Carrier has scanned the package | Progress bar with carrier milestones | None — passive wait |
| Delivered | Carrier confirms delivery | "Delivered" status, auto-advances | Proceeds to Post-Trade Rating |
| Delayed/exception | Carrier reports a delay or issue | Warning state with carrier's stated reason | View details, contact support if needed |

---

## Technical Notes

- Requires carrier tracking-webhook integration to auto-update status without manual polling — this is what makes the reassurance genuinely automatic rather than something users have to check for themselves.
