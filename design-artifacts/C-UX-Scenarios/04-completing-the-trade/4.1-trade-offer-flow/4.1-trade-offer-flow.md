## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Let Tom review and confirm the trade terms proposed by the other user — the entry point into the shared post-match completion flow used by every persona.

**User Situation:** Just got notified one of his listed books was claimed; opens the app to see what's being offered.

**Success Criteria:** Tom understands and accepts the terms in one screen, no back-and-forth needed to understand what's being proposed.

**Entry Points:**
- Push/email notification: "Your book was claimed" (from My Listings/Dashboard, 1.3)
- My Listings/Dashboard (1.3) directly, if Tom checks manually

**Exit Points:**
- Messaging (4.2) — after accepting

---

## Page Sections

### Section: Trade Summary
**Purpose:** One-glance understanding of what's being proposed — Tom's book, who claimed it, and what (if anything) is being offered in return.

### Section: Counterpart Trust Snapshot
**Purpose:** Light trust signal (rating badge) without requiring a full profile visit — enough for Tom to feel reasonably confident before accepting.

### Section: Decision
**Purpose:** Binary accept/decline — no negotiation UI on this page (negotiation, if any, happens in Messaging).

---

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Default | Page load | Trade summary + accept/decline | Accept or decline |
| Accepted | Tom accepts | Brief confirmation, auto-advances | None — proceeds to Messaging |
| Declined | Tom declines | Listing returns to "Live" status | Return to Dashboard |

---

## Technical Notes

- No address or contact info is shown on this page — that's the point. Trust snapshot only; actual coordination happens via in-platform Messaging (4.2), never exposing personal contact details.
