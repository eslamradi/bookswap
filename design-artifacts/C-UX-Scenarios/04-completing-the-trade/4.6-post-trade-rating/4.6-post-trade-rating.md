## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Close out the trade with mutual ratings — the scenario's success point, and the data source that feeds User Profile/Reputation (3.3) trust checks elsewhere in the product.

**User Situation:** Delivery just confirmed, trade is functionally complete.

**Success Criteria:** Both parties rate each other, trade is marked complete, and the resulting rating data strengthens the trust system for future trades.

**Entry Points:**
- Trade Confirmation & Tracking (4.5) — automatically, once delivery confirmed

**Exit Points:**
- None required — scenario success. Trade marked complete.

## Page Sections

### Section: Completion Banner
**Purpose:** Confirm the trade is done — emotional closure, similar in spirit to the confirmation on My Listings/Dashboard (1.3).

### Section: Rating Input
**Purpose:** Quick star rating plus optional comment — low friction, since this is a closing action, not the main task.

---

## Page States

| State | When | Appearance | Actions |
|-------|------|------------|---------|
| Default | Page load | Rating prompt, no rating selected | Select stars, optionally comment, submit |
| Submitted | Rating sent | Confirmation, shows rating received in return (if available) | Leave |
| Skipped | User navigates away without rating | Trade still marked complete; rating remains open for a limited window | Return later to rate |

---

## Technical Notes

- Rating should not block trade completion — Tom's task is "done" once delivery is confirmed regardless of whether he rates immediately; rating is encouraged, not gated.
