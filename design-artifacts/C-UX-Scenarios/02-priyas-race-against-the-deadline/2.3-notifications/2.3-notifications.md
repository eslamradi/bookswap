## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Confirm the notify-me alert is active — the scenario's success point. Success here is *certainty*, not the book itself.

**User Situation:** Just tapped "Notify me" on Search Results, wants confirmation it actually worked.

**Success Criteria:** Priya closes the app confident she'll be alerted the moment a match appears — anxiety resolved even without the book in hand yet.

**Entry Points:**
- Search Results (2.2) — after activating notify-me

**Exit Points:**
- None required — scenario success. Priya leaves the app.
- (Future session) Push/email notification → Book Listing Detail, when a match appears

## Page Sections

### Section: Confirmation
**Purpose:** Deliver the certainty payoff clearly — this IS the success state for this scenario.
**Content:** Explicit confirmation with the exact title being watched, so there's no doubt it's tracking the right book.

### Section: Alert Channel
**Purpose:** Show Priya how she'll be notified, reinforcing that she doesn't need to keep checking manually.

### Section: Manage Alerts (secondary)
**Purpose:** Low-emphasis path to view/edit all active alerts, for users tracking multiple titles.
